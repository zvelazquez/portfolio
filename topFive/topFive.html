My top fives...



My top 5 favorite musicians/artists/bands:

  1. The Strokes

    2. I love this band because it is the type of sound I aspire to create sonically, and every single song by them is so good



  2. Wolf Alice

    3. I love this band because I love the fluidity of their sound, and I think that the lead singer, Ellie Rowsell, has one of the most beautiful singing voices I have ever heard.



  3. The Voidz

    4. I discovered this band as a result of loving The Strokes, because this is the side band of Strokes singer Julian Casablancas. Their sound is much more experimental and grungy



  4. Arctic Monkeys

    5. I love how much this band's sound has changed and adapted over the years, and I think that Alex Turner has written some of the best lyrics ever.



  5. Father John Misty

    6. FJM's music is a bit more different to what I listen to regularly, but his ability to blend genre's and appeal to different tastes while exploring lyrical meaning has made him one of my favorites.
